About Me


I am currently a student at KU- learning coding and full stack web development. After several years of trying to decide what I wanted to do- I finally picked something and am now pursuing the career I had always envisioned for myself. I decided to go into this industry because I love creating and the opportunities it offers. I am always striving to improve myself and bring 100% to the work that I do.

I love traveling in my spare time and going to concerts. Music is life to me. I paint and also enjoy photography. I have a dachshund/terrier mix named Herman that I adopted from a rescue. I collect and like rebuilding vintage bicycles. Before I started at KU for my current goal, I attended Metropolitan Community Colleges in Kansas city. I majored in photography and minored in sculpture. I won best of show several years in a row at the annual art show. I also participated in showings with the Kansas City Art Institute.

I've worn many hats in the past several years. I worked at a tattoo shop in Kansas City for over a year and then moved to Topeka, KS. I started working for Clinique Cosmetics as a makeup artist and stayed with them for two years. After Clinique I worked in law enforcement as a 911 Dispatcher and then a Corrections Officer.

My current employer at the VA has always rated me as exceptional (the highest you can be rated) every year that i've been there. I am going on six years in this position but have decided that I need to move on to bigger and better things. I need to start a career. I'm passionate about technology and love trying new things.
